# GUMGENIE QUALITY CRITERIA & SCORING RUBRIC
*North-star standards for generated digital products*

---

## OVERVIEW

This rubric defines the quality standards for all GumGenie-generated digital products. Every generated product should score **80+ points** across the four core dimensions to be considered "launch-ready."

**Scoring Scale**: 0-100 points per dimension
- **90-100**: Exceptional (market-leading quality)
- **80-89**: Professional (ready to launch)
- **70-79**: Good (needs minor polish)
- **60-69**: Adequate (needs significant improvement)
- **Below 60**: Poor (major revision required)

---

## DIMENSION 1: CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION (25 points)

### **Headline & Value Proposition (8 points)**
- **8 pts**: Specific, benefit-focused headline with clear transformation promise
- **6 pts**: Clear headline with some specificity, moderate benefit clarity
- **4 pts**: Generic headline, unclear value proposition
- **2 pts**: Vague or confusing headline
- **0 pts**: Missing or poor headline

**Benchmark Examples:**
- ✅ "500+ Production-Ready AI Prompts That Generate $10K+ Content in 30 Minutes"
- ❌ "AI Prompts for Everyone"

### **Social Proof & Credibility (6 points)**
- **6 pts**: Specific numbers, testimonials, screenshots, or results
- **4 pts**: Some proof elements but lacking specificity
- **2 pts**: Generic social proof claims
- **0 pts**: No social proof

### **Pricing Strategy & Psychology (6 points)**
- **6 pts**: Strategic pricing with clear tiers, urgency, and value anchoring
- **4 pts**: Good pricing structure with some psychological elements
- **2 pts**: Basic pricing, minimal optimization
- **0 pts**: Poor or confusing pricing

### **Call-to-Action Optimization (5 points)**
- **5 pts**: Action-oriented, specific, creates urgency ("Unlock Master-Level Output")
- **3 pts**: Clear CTA with some urgency
- **1 pt**: Basic "Buy Now" CTA
- **0 pts**: Weak or missing CTA

---

## DIMENSION 2: VISUAL DESIGN (25 points)

### **Visual Hierarchy & Layout (8 points)**
- **8 pts**: Perfect information flow, clear sections, excellent readability
- **6 pts**: Good structure with minor flow issues
- **4 pts**: Adequate structure, some confusion
- **2 pts**: Poor organization, hard to follow
- **0 pts**: No clear structure

### **Brand Consistency & Aesthetics (7 points)**
- **7 pts**: Cohesive color scheme, typography, consistent style throughout
- **5 pts**: Mostly consistent with minor variations
- **3 pts**: Some consistency issues
- **1 pt**: Inconsistent styling
- **0 pts**: No visual consistency

### **Mobile & Accessibility (5 points)**
- **5 pts**: Perfect mobile responsiveness, WCAG AA compliance
- **3 pts**: Good mobile experience, basic accessibility
- **1 pt**: Some mobile issues, poor accessibility
- **0 pts**: Not mobile-friendly

### **Professional Polish (5 points)**
- **5 pts**: High-end finish, attention to detail, premium feel
- **3 pts**: Professional appearance with minor imperfections
- **1 pt**: Basic design, lacks polish
- **0 pts**: Amateur appearance

---

## DIMENSION 3: MONETIZATION DEPTH (25 points)

### **Value Stack Architecture (10 points)**
- **10 pts**: Multi-tier pricing with clear value progression (Starter/Pro/Ultimate)
- **8 pts**: Good tier structure with decent differentiation
- **6 pts**: Basic tiering, some value clarity
- **4 pts**: Poor tier differentiation
- **0 pts**: Single pricing or unclear value

**Required Elements:**
- 3 pricing tiers minimum
- Clear feature differentiation per tier
- Value-based pricing (not cost-plus)
- Upsell/cross-sell opportunities

### **Revenue Optimization (8 points)**
- **8 pts**: Multiple revenue streams, lifetime value optimization, ecosystem thinking
- **6 pts**: Good revenue strategy with some optimization
- **4 pts**: Basic monetization, minimal optimization
- **2 pts**: Single revenue stream
- **0 pts**: Poor monetization strategy

### **Market Positioning (7 points)**
- **7 pts**: Clear competitive advantage, unique selling proposition, specific niche
- **5 pts**: Good positioning with some differentiation
- **3 pts**: Basic positioning, limited differentiation
- **1 pt**: Generic positioning
- **0 pts**: No clear positioning

---

## DIMENSION 4: COMPLETENESS & DELIVERABILITY (25 points)

### **Content Quality & Depth (10 points)**
- **10 pts**: Comprehensive, actionable, immediate value, professional grade
- **8 pts**: Good depth with minor gaps
- **6 pts**: Adequate content, some value
- **4 pts**: Basic content, limited value
- **0 pts**: Poor or incomplete content

### **Technical Implementation (8 points)**
- **8 pts**: Perfect file formats, optimized sizes, cross-platform compatibility
- **6 pts**: Good technical quality with minor issues
- **4 pts**: Basic technical implementation
- **2 pts**: Some technical problems
- **0 pts**: Poor technical quality

### **Documentation & Support (7 points)**
- **7 pts**: Complete setup guides, FAQ, examples, troubleshooting
- **5 pts**: Good documentation with minor gaps
- **3 pts**: Basic documentation
- **1 pt**: Minimal documentation
- **0 pts**: No supporting materials

---

## QUALITY GATES & LAUNCH CRITERIA

### **Minimum Launch Standards**
- **Total Score**: 80+ points (across all dimensions)
- **No dimension below**: 15 points
- **Critical requirements**: All must be present:
  - Clear value proposition
  - 3-tier pricing structure
  - Professional visual design
  - Complete deliverables
  - Mobile-responsive layout

### **Quality Assurance Checklist**
- [ ] Value proposition tested with target audience
- [ ] Pricing validated against market research
- [ ] Visual design reviewed for consistency
- [ ] Technical files tested on target platforms
- [ ] Documentation reviewed for completeness
- [ ] Mobile experience validated
- [ ] Accessibility standards met (WCAG AA minimum)
