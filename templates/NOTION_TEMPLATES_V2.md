## 1. MARKET STRATEGY & POSITIONING

### **Target Market Analysis**

#### **Primary Audience**
- **Demographics**: Knowledge workers 25-45, entrepreneurs, small business owners, productivity enthusiasts
- **Psychographics**: Overwhelmed by information scattered across tools, seeking organized systems, values visual organization
- **Market Size**: 50M+ Notion users globally, 15M+ paid subscribers, growing 200%+ annually
- **Buying Behavior**: Price-sensitive but values time savings, prefers templates over custom building, researches thoroughly

#### **Secondary Markets**
- **Teams & Agencies**: Small teams (5-15 people) needing collaborative workspaces
- **Students & Academics**: Research organization, thesis management, academic planning
- **Content Creators**: Content calendars, idea management, project tracking
- **Consultants & Freelancers**: Client management, project delivery, knowledge bases

#### **Market Validation**
- **Search Volume**: "Notion templates" (450K monthly), "productivity dashboard" (180K monthly)
- **Competitor Analysis**: Prototion ($45 average), Template Gallery ($29 average), NotionEverything ($35 average)
- **Customer Interviews**: Top pain points: "takes weeks to build", "doesn't look professional", "breaks when I customize"
