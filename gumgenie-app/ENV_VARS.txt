Free-tier notes (verify against docs; quotas can change)
- Pages Functions requests count toward Workers Free plan: 100,000 requests/day (resets at midnight UTC)
- KV Free plan: 100,000 reads/day; 1,000 writes/day to different keys; 1 GB storage
- D1 Free plan: 5M rows read/day; 100k rows written/day; 5 GB storage
